Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about exploring Shoolini, campus life, and events.

General

Where is Shoolini University located?

Shoolini University is located near Solan in Himachal Pradesh, surrounded by hills and pine forests, about 60–70 km from Chandigarh.

How can I reach the campus?

You can reach the campus by road from Solan or Dharampur via taxi, cab, or local bus, and the nearest major railway station and airport are at Chandigarh.

Do I need prior permission to visit the campus?

Visitors are usually allowed during working hours, but it is recommended to inform the admission or reception desk in advance, especially for group visits.

Campus Experience

What are the must‑visit spots on campus?

Popular spots include the central plaza, library area, hostels with valley views, sports grounds, and various themed corners featured on the Attractions page.

Can I explore the campus on my own?

Visitors can walk around most common areas, but some academic or residential zones may be restricted, so always follow directions from university staff or security.

Is there a campus map available?

A basic campus layout is highlighted on the homepage and Attractions sections, and visitors can request an updated map or guidance at the main reception.

Events & Culture

How can I know about upcoming events?

Major fests, cultural shows, and competitions are listed on the Events page and are also announced through official university channels and social media.

Can visitors attend university events?

Many flagship events are open to guests, but some require prior registration, passes, or invitations, so always check details on the Events page.

Are there student clubs and societies on campus?

The campus hosts multiple clubs for culture, technical activities, entrepreneurship, and sports, which actively participate in events and festivals round the year.

Stay & Facilities

Is on‑campus accommodation available for visitors?

Limited guest accommodation may be available for official visitors and parents during specific occasions, and it usually needs to be booked in advance.

What facilities can visitors access?

Visitors can generally access cafeterias, some open seating areas, and designated viewing points while academic buildings and hostels remain restricted.

Is photography allowed on campus?

Personal photography in open areas is usually fine, but professional shoots or drone usage may require special permission from the administration.

Admissions & Contact

Where can I get information about courses and admissions?

Detailed information is available on the official Shoolini University website, and the admissions office can guide you on programs, eligibility, and application process.

How can I contact the university for visit‑related queries?

You can reach the university through the contact details provided on the official website or by calling the main reception for visit timings and guidance.
